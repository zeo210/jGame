jGame! Play every round like Final Jeopardy!

A simple Jeopardy! game where you wager points and answer questions!

Two styles of game modes. Normal and practice.

Play normal mode to answer questions in a timed environment with other players. Play practice mode to answer questions at your own pace!

Put the game on the big screen! Group play without seperate devices!

Follow along the normal mode as a spectator. Perfect for groups that want to play in person.

Leaderboard. Track and compare your progress.

Find where you stand in the points leaderboard.
